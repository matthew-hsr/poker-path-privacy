# Privacy Policy for PokerPath

**Last updated: July 19, 2026**

## Overview

PokerPath ("the App") is a simulated poker game for entertainment purposes only. No real money is wagered or exchanged. This policy describes how we handle your information.

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored on Your Device
- **Game progress**: Your bankroll balance and session statistics are stored locally on your device using standard platform storage (UserDefaults on iOS, SharedPreferences on Android). This data never leaves your device.

### Advertising
- The App uses **Google AdMob** to display ads. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve ads, including:
  - Device identifiers
  - IP address
  - General location (country/region)
  - App usage data

For more details, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Crash Reporting
- The App uses **Firebase Crashlytics** (Google) to collect crash reports. When the App crashes, Crashlytics automatically sends a report containing the device model, OS version, app version, and a stack trace. This data is used solely to identify and fix bugs. No personally identifiable information is included in crash reports. For more details, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Cookies and Identifiers
- Google AdMob may use cookies or similar technologies (the IDFA on iOS, the Advertising ID on Android) to identify your device for advertising and frequency capping.

### App Tracking Transparency (iOS)
- On iOS 14.5 and later, the App will ask for your permission before tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites for advertising purposes.
- If you decline, ads will still be shown but they will not be personalized.

### Android Advertising ID
- On Android, you can reset or opt out of personalized advertising at any time from your device's system settings (Settings → Google → Ads).

## Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect your name, email, or any personal contact information.
- We do not require account creation or login.
- We do not collect or transmit your gameplay data to any server.
- We do not use analytics services beyond what AdMob and Firebase Crashlytics provide.

## Data Sharing
- We do not sell your data to third parties.
- Ad-related data is shared only with Google AdMob as described above.

## Children's Privacy
- The App is rated 17+ due to simulated gambling content and is not intended for children under 17.
- We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Data Retention
- All game data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the App removes all stored data.

## Security
The security of your data is important to us. We use standard platform security features to protect information stored locally on your device. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

## Your Data Rights
Depending on your location, you may have the right to access or delete the data we hold. Since all game data is stored locally, you can exercise these rights at any time by resetting your progress in the App settings or deleting the App from your device.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
